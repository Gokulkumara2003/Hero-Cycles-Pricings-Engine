Problem Statement:
Hero Cycles is one of the largest bicycle manufacturers in India. You are a fresher engineer joining their small digital team. On Day 1 your manager gives you this brief:

“We sell thousands of cycle configurations — different frames, different gear sets, different tyre types. Our sales team is constantly going back and forth on pricing because part costs change every few months — a tyre priced at 200 in January may be 230 by December. Right now everything is done on Excel sheets. We need a proper pricing engine — something salesperson(s) can use to manage cycle configurations & parts, and instantly get the total price, broken down by component. Can you build this?”

Questions araised while solving:

1.What are the components that make up a cycle configuration?

2.Can a cycle have multiple variants?

3.How should the selling price be calculated?

4.What are the REST APIs required?

5.What should my frontend include?

6.Design of frontend?

7.Different types of frames?

8.Different types of cycles?

9.Different types of gear sets?


Assumptions made:

1.Every cycle configuration has unique name.

2.Component cost is always positive.

3.Selling price is calculated by Type of cycle and component used in cycle.
